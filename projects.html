Projects

OEG

I developed online explanation generation (OEG) for human-robot teaming, reducing cognitive load by incrementally delivering model-reconciling justifications during task execution. Evaluated across domains with strong empirical results

PEG

I introduced progressive explanation generation (PEG) for human-robot teaming, modeling explanation order preferences via inverse reinforcement learning to reduce cognitive load and improve interpretability during task execution.

NOVA

I Built a VR-guided robotic system that simulates and validates manipulation plans before execution. By previewing actions in virtual space, it ensures safety, reduces risk in high-precision tasks, and enables intuitive human-in-the-loop control.

MOSAIC

I developed a web-based AI facilitation platform for distributed teams managing biased, asymmetric information. It guides conversations to reduce noise, surface shared context, and support aligned, robust decision-making.
