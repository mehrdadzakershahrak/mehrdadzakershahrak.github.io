This Privacy Policy explains how Mehrdad Zaker and this website collect, use, and protect information when you visit the site, sign in with Google, or use interactive demos.

## Information We Collect

We may collect:

- contact information you choose to provide, such as your email address when you reach out
- account information returned by Google Sign-In, such as your name, email address, Google account identifier, and profile image
- technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, approximate geolocation, referring pages, and usage logs
- information you submit through demo chat, forms, or other interactive site features

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- operate, maintain, and secure the website
- authenticate users and manage signed-in sessions
- provide access to demo experiences and related features
- respond to inquiries and business requests
- analyze site reliability, performance, and usage
- improve content, products, services, and user experience
- detect abuse, fraud, unauthorized access, or policy violations

## Google Sign-In

If you sign in with Google, the site uses Google Identity Services to authenticate you. We request only the basic account information needed to identify your account and operate access-controlled features.

We do not ask for Gmail, Google Drive, Calendar, or other extended Google data scopes through this login flow.

## Demo Chat and Submitted Content

If you use a demo chat or interactive workflow, the prompts, responses, and related page context may be processed by backend systems and upstream model or automation providers in order to return results.

Do not submit confidential, regulated, export-controlled, or highly sensitive personal information unless you have a separate written agreement covering that use.

## Cookies and Session Data

This site may use cookies or similar technologies for:

- sign-in session management
- security and fraud prevention
- remembering basic user state
- analytics and performance monitoring

If you sign in, an authenticated session cookie may be stored in your browser to keep you signed in across relevant site features.

## Sharing of Information

Information may be shared with trusted infrastructure, hosting, analytics, authentication, and AI service providers to operate the site and its features.

We may also disclose information:

- to comply with legal obligations
- to enforce site policies or protect rights and security
- in connection with a business transfer, restructuring, or acquisition

We do not sell personal information in the ordinary course of operating this site.

## Data Retention

Information is retained for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the site, provide requested services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

## Third-Party Services

This site may link to or rely on third-party services, including Google and other infrastructure providers. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

## Your Choices

You may:

- avoid using sign-in features if you do not wish to authenticate with Google
- refrain from submitting sensitive information through demo tools
- contact us to request updates or deletion where appropriate and legally available

## Children's Privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through the public site.
