About / Expertise

Private AI systems, built from the workflow back.

Useful AI is rarely a generic assistant. It has a clear job, a reliable data path, and a way to prove when the answer is right.

I build AI systems for teams that need more than a demo. The work sits where data, models, rules, and daily tasks meet. One project might be a RAG pipeline over sensitive files. Another might be a source-backed assistant, a private LLM, a tuned model, or a test suite for launch.

The common thread is control. I help teams decide what data enters context. I help keep citations attached. I help choose where inference runs, what failures must be measured, and how the interface fits the work. That can mean private VPC or on-prem design, prototype hardening, better search and ranking, tool integration, model routing, or review flows that need human signoff.

My background spans foundation models, RAG, agentic workflows, ranking and recommendation systems, robotics-adjacent AI, reinforcement learning, model serving, and platform reliability. The stack may include LangChain, LlamaIndex, vLLM, Ollama, llama.cpp, MLX, vector databases, Kubernetes, private cloud networking, answer tests, and source review tools. I choose tools around the operating constraint, not the trend.

The best fit is a team in a regulated, document-heavy, or complex setting. That can mean healthcare, finance, legal, manufacturing, enterprise operations, robotics, or internal knowledge work. These teams need answers tied to evidence. They need data kept inside known bounds. They need systems that can be watched after launch. The goal is AI that earns trust through behavior, not positioning.

Why private AI

Why teams choose private AI deployments

Private AI is not only about where a model runs. It is about data exposure, governance, cost visibility, tests, and control over sensitive work.

Gartner reported a hard pattern. By the end of 2025, at least half of GenAI projects were abandoned after proof of concept because of poor data, weak risk controls, rising costs, or unclear value.
