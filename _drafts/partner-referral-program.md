# Mehrdad Zaker Referral Partner Program

## Program overview

This is a private, handpicked referral program for partners who work closely with document-heavy operators and see recurring workflow pain around PDFs, review time, follow-up consistency, and non-technical automation needs.

The program is not a public affiliate program and it is not self-serve. The goal is simple: if you know clients who are a strong fit, make a warm introduction and Mehrdad handles the rest.

## Best-fit referrals

The strongest referrals usually come from teams where the work starts with documents, not software-first buyers.

Examples:

- real estate operators, brokers, transaction-oriented advisors, and people working across leases, diligence binders, listing packets, inspection reports, and deal files
- RFQ, procurement, proposal, and vendor-response teams dealing with RFPs, RFQs, statements of work, pricing sheets, and compliance documents
- sales and operations teams working with quotes, proposals, contracts, onboarding packets, policy documents, or recurring administrative PDFs
- adjacent document-heavy teams that need faster review, better consistency, and source-backed answers without requiring a technical team to drive adoption

## What Mehrdad and IDX help with

The entry point is usually document workflow pain, but the work can expand into the broader delivery stack where it makes sense.

- IDX:
  source-grounded document workflows for uploaded PDFs, with source-backed answers, search, and viewer-linked review
- Custom AI Systems:
  workflow-specific automation built around the same use case when the client needs more than document search and review
- Private AI Deployment:
  architecture and delivery guidance when privacy, security, deployment, or infrastructure constraints become central

The best fit is a client with a real workflow owner, a recurring document problem, and a business reason to move faster without losing traceability.

## Qualification checklist

Good referrals usually have most of the following:

- repetitive document review, comparison, extraction, or follow-up work
- meaningful volume of PDFs, contracts, RFQs, listing packets, proposals, or similar files
- a real operator, manager, or decision-maker who feels the pain directly
- pressure around speed, consistency, traceability, defensibility, or workflow automation
- willingness to join a short discovery call

Usually not a fit:

- people looking for a generic chatbot with no concrete workflow
- buyers expecting a self-serve SaaS setup with no discovery
- prospects with no document volume, no workflow owner, or no operational urgency

## How referrals work

The partner role is intro-only.

1. The partner identifies a client or operator who looks like a fit.
2. The partner makes a warm email introduction.
3. Mehrdad owns discovery, demo, scoping, proposal, close, and onboarding.
4. The partner may join follow-up calls if strategically useful, but that is optional.

The partner is not expected to sell technical architecture, promise product behavior, or run delivery.

## Compensation

- 10% referral fee on the first paid contract only
- payout is made after client payment is received
- applies only to closed business directly attributable to the partner introduction

## Attribution and exclusions

- attribution window: 6 months from the first warm email introduction
- excluded: existing active pipeline, existing direct conversations, duplicate introductions after another partner or direct source, and unpaid pilots that never convert
- no stacking with other referral sources or overlapping commissions

If there is any ambiguity about attribution, it should be clarified early, before substantial sales work begins.

## What to say

Good partner framing:

- “This may help your team review document-heavy work faster without losing the original source page.”
- “This is a practical workflow conversation, not a generic AI demo.”
- “If the fit is there, Mehrdad will handle discovery and show the right starting path.”

Avoid saying:

- “It runs your workflow autonomously.”
- “It is a plug-and-play self-serve setup.”
- “It replaces expert review.”

The safe promise is practical workflow review, source-backed answers, and founder-led discovery around the client’s real operating constraints.
