Open with the specific production problem, who is facing it, and why the page exists. Keep service positioning out of the article body unless it helps the reader choose the next step.

## Why This Matters

Explain the operational constraint in plain terms. Add citations for public claims about risk, adoption, cost, security, standards, or benchmarks.

## How To Approach It

Break the work into implementation decisions, tradeoffs, and checks. Use examples when they clarify the decision, but avoid exact private engagement metrics unless they are verified for public use.
