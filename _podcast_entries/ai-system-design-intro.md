This episode opens a practical conversation about AI system design: how promising ideas move from research and prototypes into systems that can survive real use. It sets up the larger theme that strong models alone are not enough without structure, deployment discipline, and clear product thinking.

It is a useful starting point for teams thinking about architecture, workflow fit, and the difference between a compelling demo and a system that can keep earning trust over time.
