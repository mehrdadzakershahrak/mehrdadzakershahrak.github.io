What Is IDX?

IDX adds source-grounded PDF workflows to ChatGPT

IDX helps teams work with uploaded PDFs, ask focused questions, and verify answers against source pages. Under the hood, IDX can connect document workflows to ChatGPT through MCP.

/idx/assistant/ is the public workflow-guidance page on this site. The credentialed website flow lives on /login/ and /idx/dashboard/. ChatGPT-connected MCP testing belongs on https://idx.mehrdadzaker.com, not on this guidance page. Contact IDX support if you want help with workflow fit, dashboard setup, or ChatGPT-connected IDX.
