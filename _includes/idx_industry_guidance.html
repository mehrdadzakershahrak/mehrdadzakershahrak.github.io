Why IDX

A document workflow that stays closer to the evidence.

Source-backed reading

Pages, citations, and matched excerpts stay in the same surface so you can audit the answer instead of trusting a summary in isolation.

Structured review

Search, query, lint, graph, insights, and delta modes all operate on the same library instead of fragmenting your review path across tools.

Recoverable ingest
