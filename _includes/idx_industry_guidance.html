Objection Proof

Questions buyers usually need answered before they trust a document AI workflow.

Can I verify answers?

That is the core IDX workflow. Important answers should carry citations, matched excerpts, or viewer links so the reviewer can open the source page before acting.

What happens when ingest fails?

Document and compile states are visible. A stalled or failed document should be diagnosable as a workflow state instead of disappearing into an opaque queue.

Where do uploads live?

Authenticated uploads, indexing, search, viewer links, and workspace state run on idx.mehrdadzaker.com. The public page is informational and the dashboard/login routes hand off to the product host.

Is this replacing expert review?

No. IDX is a document workflow and retrieval tool. It helps reviewers find, compare, and verify evidence, but users remain responsible for validating outputs before relying on them.

When should I contact support?

Contact support for sign-in, session, upload, indexing, readiness, search, viewer-link, workflow-fit, or operational questions specific to IDX.
