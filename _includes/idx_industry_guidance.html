Why IDX

Built to fix the document failures that make AI hard to trust.

Grounding that holds up

Pages, citations, and matched excerpts stay in the same surface. The answer stays tied to its evidence.

Verification in the same workflow

Search, query, graph, insight, and delta views use the same library. Reviewers can check claims without leaving the flow.

Ingest you can diagnose

Processing, compile, stalled, and failure states are visible. Document workflows do not vanish into an opaque queue.
